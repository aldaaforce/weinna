Problem Space: We have embarked on the development of a food delivery application catering specifically to the elderly demographic. Our motivation stems from recognizing an unmet need within the current landscape of food delivery services. In a world where food delivery has become a commonplace convenience, we've observed that existing apps often fall short in catering to the unique needs of older users. It's crucial to ensure that something as vital as nourishment and self-care remains accessible to everyone, regardless of age.

Elderly individuals frequently contend with distinct challenges, such as managing medical conditions, food allergies, and adhering to specific dietary requirements. Our belief is that consolidating all these crucial aspects into a single, user-friendly app can greatly benefit this demographic. There are instances when leaving one's home is not a viable option, making the creation of an intuitive food delivery app a novel and worthwhile endeavor.
