About me!

I’m Weinna, and I like to design clean and beautiful things. Design is a way of expressing my love for authenticity and an open-minded approach to life. I believe in the power of thoughtful design to communicate ideas, evoke emotions, and make a positive difference in the world.

I started out in graphic design, where I honed my skills in visual storytelling and aesthetics. I’m now progressing towards product design, driven by a desire to create meaningful and user-centric solutions.

In my spare time, you can find me practicing piano improv, playing games with my cousins, enjoying a cup of coffee at a café, or taking a long walk by the lake in Port Credit.
