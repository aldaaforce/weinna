ProjectPals

View Prototype

By Janie Ong, Sumalfka Liu, Sophia Zhu, Weinna Zheng

Introducing ProjectPals, a virtual platform that allows you to find ideas to start side projects and collaborate with other like-minded individuals! Created for the Ctrl + y designathon in September 2023 at the University of Waterloo.

Tools Timeline My Role
FigJam 2 days total UX Research
Figma Research: 6 hours Visual Design
Design: 1 day UX Design

Prompt: Create a versatile solution that empowers job seekers or independent workers to find purposeful projects, cultivate meaningful networks, and navigate the intricacies of the current job market. The proposed solution should include the use of emerging technologies (such as generative AI).

Problem Space: There are a lack of virtual spaces for people interested in side projects, both professionally and personally, as they struggle to find resources and connect with others.
